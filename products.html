A Collection in Bloom

Each piece you see here begins with the land — oils slowly infused by hand, wax poured with intention, herbs gathered at their peak.

This Catalog is a quiet invitation to explore our collection: gentle skincare, earth-toned candles, and soaps steeped in scent and season.

Rooted in tradition and guided by care, every item is crafted to bring a bit of stillness, beauty, and balance to the everyday.

Herbal Skincare

Tended by hand. Guided by the land.

Every balm, blend, and tonic begins in the garden — or somewhere nearby, where the wild still thrives. I infuse each batch slowly, often over days or weeks, letting herbs release their essence in their own time. The result is simple skincare: grounded in tradition, gentle on the skin, and crafted to bring a little peace to your daily rhythm.

Sun in a Jar

A golden balm for weathered hands and quiet rituals.

Slowly infused with calendula blossoms gathered under high summer sun, this balm is a gentle healer. its warmth melts into skin, soothing cracks and softening calluses with every use. Perfect for dry knuckles, elbows, or anywhere the day leaves its mark. Lightly scented by th herbs themselves — earthy, sweet, and sunlit.

Pathway Steam

Open the senses. Return to breath.

A sacred bowl of herbs and petals — chamomile, rose, yarrow, and lavender — meant to be steeped and inhaled. Use as a facial steam, a ritual opening, or simply a pause in the woods of your day. This blend draws you back into the body, inviting stillness, clearing fog, and warming the heartspace.

Wildflower Toner

A dewdrop for your skin.

Distilled from petals and leaves gathered at their most fragrant, this toner brings clarity and calm to freshly cleansed skin. Spritz or sweep — it settles the senses and prepares the face for nourishment. Hints of rose, yarrow, and mint drift like breeze over meadow. Best used when you've just woken or just come home.

Candles

Wax, wick, and quiet moments.

Each candle is hand-poured in small batches, using natural waxes and plant-based scents that echo the seasons. I blend each fragrance to feel like a place — a time of day, a breath in the woods, a feeling you didn't know you missed. These aren't just scents; they're stories, lit slowly and burned with care.

Hearthlight

Where quiet moments glow.

Poured for evenings when the world softens. Hearthlight is a grounding blend of cedarwood, clove, and dried orange peel — slow-burning and warm, like conversation by firelight. Each jar is made in small batches, hand-labeled, and stirred with intention.

Harvest Tapers

Hand-dipped light for home and hearth.

Simple and steady, these tapers are made in small seasonal batches using natural beeswax and a slow-pull method passed down through generations. Each one burns with a warm, honeyed glow — perfect for the dinner table, the mantle, or any quiet corner that calls for a little handmade light. Burn Evenly. Burn often.

Wishing Bowl

Light for what's yet to come.

Hand-poured into a handmade clay vessel and adorned with dried herbs and flowers, this three-wick candle is meant for intention-setting and slow evenings. Notes of fir needle, lavender, and sweet marjoram rise as it burns — grounding and gently clarifying. Place it beside a journal, a bath, or a quiet threshold of your own making.

Soap

From earth to lather

Each bar is a quiet act of care — hand-poured in small batches using plant-based oils, wild herbs, and age-old methods. These soaps invite you into daily ritual: grounding, cleansing, and gently reconnecting you to what's real. skin-softening, soul-steadying, and made with the same calm intention that carries through all we do.

Lemon Balm & Oat

Bright mornings, softened skin.

This gentle, cold-processed bar blends calming lemon balm with finely ground oats for a softly exfoliating, skin-loving cleanse. Subtly citrusy with herbal warmth, it's the kind of scent that feels like sunlight through linen. Ideal for sensitive or dry skin, and made for slow, grounding rituals — whether at the start of day or winding it down.

Lavender & Clay

For quiet evenings and clear skin.

This calming bar blends floral lavender essential oil with the purifying properties of kaolin clay. Topped with dried buds and swirled with a soft purple hue, it's as soothing to the senses as it is to the skin. Perfect for winding down — or for anyone who needs a moment of stillness in the everyday.

Forest Ritual

Deep woods. Deep breath.

Crafted with wild rosemary, lavender, and mineral-rich spirulina, this dark green bar carries the spirit of the forest floor. Earthy, herbal, and invigorating, it's designed to awaken the senses while nourishing the skin. Use it when you need to come back to yourself — when your body aches for cool moss, river stone, and stillness
